Why Redesign National Flags for Democracy and Inclusion Matters

The movement for Redesign National Flags for Democracy and Inclusion is transforming classrooms and communities by creating symbols that everyone can identify with. Outdated flags featuring religious or exclusive symbols are being reimagined to represent modern, diverse societies.

Classroom Guides: Teaching with Redesign National Flags for Democracy and Inclusion

  • Lesson 1: Learn the alphabet through flag symbols — use Redesign National Flags for Democracy and Inclusion to teach early literacy and foster civic pride.
  • Lesson 2: Explore how Redesign National Flags for Democracy and Inclusion can bridge cultural divides and encourage respect for diversity.
  • Lesson 3: Challenge students to design their own flag concepts using the principles of Redesign National Flags for Democracy and Inclusion.
